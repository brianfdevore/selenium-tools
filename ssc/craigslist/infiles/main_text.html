Replacement/Forestry Door Slugs

Tired of breaking the glass on the front door of your skid steer, excavator, backhoe, dozer, or wheel loader?  Or maybe, you're doing forestry or demolition work and you'd like to keep your operator safe.  Either way, we've got you covered with our replacement/forestry door slugs.

These bad boys are precision manufactured with polycarbonate plastic to match the exact size and specs of the factory glass in your door.  They are 1/2 inch thick, except for the outermost perimeter where they are milled down to about 1/4 inch thick so that they will install in the same molding as your old glass door.

Say goodbye to broken glass, and hello to extreme safety and durability, visit our website at the link below to see if we have a replacement/forestry door slug for your machine:
